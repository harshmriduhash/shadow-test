# ShadowTest: Business Case & Financial Projections (1 Year)

## 💎 The Market Opportunity
ShadowTest occupies a high-growth niche in the "AI-Native DevOps" and "UX Intelligence" markets. Traditional testing (Selenium/Cypress) is brittle; ShadowTest is resilient because it thinks like a human. 

**Product-Market Fit:** B2B SaaS companies with complex user flows (Fintech, Healthcare, E-commerce) where a single broken flow costs thousands in lost conversion.

---

## 📈 1-Year Revenue Projection

We assume a 3-tier SaaS pricing model:
- **Starter:** $49/mo (5 simulations/mo)
- **Growth:** $199/mo (30 simulations/mo)
- **Enterprise:** $999/mo (Unlimited + Priority Support)

## 🏦 Net Take-Home (Financial Summary)

- **Total Gross Revenue (Year 1):** $285,000
- **Total Operational Expenses:** ($65,400)
- **EBITDA (Profit before Tax):** $219,600
- **Estimated Taxes (25% avg):** ($54,900)
- **Net Take-Home Profit:** **$164,700**

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Profit Margin:** ~57%
> This is a high-margin software business. The primary lever for increasing profit is optimizing the **AI Token Efficiency** (reducing input tokens for simulations).

## 🚀 Future Upside
As the model matures, Enterprise contracts (SOC2 compliance packages) can push the ACV (Average Contract Value) from $100 to over $2,000 per user, significantly accelerating revenue without linear cost increases.
