# ShadowTest: Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy

## 🚀 The Launch Philosophy
Don't launch to "everyone." ShadowTest is a high-trust tool. Your first users should be **VPEs, CTOs, and Product Leads** at Series A/B startups who are tired of Cypress tests breaking.

---

## 🎯 Acquisition Strategy

### Phase 1: The "Inner Circle" (First 20 Beta Customers)
- **Founder-Led Sales:** No ads yet. Use LinkedIn and Twitter (X) to DM CTOs. 
- **The Hook:** "I built an AI that tests your UX behavior and finds bugs you didn't know existed. Can I run it on your site for free and give you the report?"
- **Goal:** Get 20 high-quality logos and testimonials.

### Phase 2: Scaling to 100+ Users
- **Content as a Moat:** Write "The Death of Cypress" or "Why AI Is Scarier Than Your QA Team" on Substack/Medium.
- **Product Hunt Launch:** Coordinate a massive Day 1 launch once the 20 beta users have provided feedback.
- **SEO:** Target keywords like "AI autonomous testing" and "LLM behavioral QA."

---

## 📅 45-Day Execution Roadmap

### **Weeks 1-2: Alpha Outreach & Feedback**
*   **Day 1-3:** Identify 50 "Target" SaaS companies on LinkedIn.
*   **Day 4-7:** Send custom outreach DMs. Offer 1 "AI Simulation Audit" for free.
*   **Day 8-14:** Run simulations for the first 5 respondents. Refine the **Report** output based on their feedback.

### **Weeks 3-4: Beta Program & Content**
*   **Day 15-20:** Automate the onboarding for the next 15 beta users. 
*   **Day 21-25:** Write 2 deep-dive case studies from Alpha results (e.g., "How ShadowTest found a $10k/mo leak in a checkout flow").
*   **Day 26-30:** Post case studies on Hacker News and Reddit (r/saas, r/webdev).

### **Weeks 5-6: Scaling & Pre-Launch**
*   **Day 31-35:** Fix all UX bugs reported by beta users. 
*   **Day 36-40:** Reach out to "Product Hunt" hunters for a featured launch.
*   **Day 41-45:** Social Media Blitz. Announce the "Public Beta" with a limited-time 50% discount for the first 100 users.

---

## 💰 Marketing & Ads: Should you spend?
**No.** Not for the first 45 days. 
- **Ads** are for scaling a proven funnel. 
- **Organic Outreach** is for finding product-market fit. 
Spend $0 on ads until you have 5 paying customers who say *"I can't live without this."*
